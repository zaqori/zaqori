FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is ZAQORI really free?

Yes. All 20 simulators and 50+ articles are completely free. No signup, no paywall, no premium tier. We may add non-intrusive ads to support the site in the future.

How accurate are the simulations?

Our simulators use widely accepted mathematical models (compound interest, exponential growth, calorie balance, etc.) with realistic assumptions. They are designed for planning and motivation, not as exact predictions.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All calculations run entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data you enter into the simulators.

Can I share my results?

Yes! Every simulator has a share button. You can copy a permalink to your specific results, or share via the native share dialog on mobile.

How do I get the most out of ZAQORI?

Start with a single area you want to improve — finances, fitness, or learning. Run multiple scenarios. Then commit to one small change and revisit the simulator in a few months to see the impact.
