Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Our Privacy Promise in Plain English

ZAQORI exists to help you see the long-term impact of your choices. We don't need your data to do that. In fact, every simulator on this site runs entirely in your browser. We literally cannot see the numbers you enter.

This page describes what little data we do collect (mostly aggregate, anonymous stuff) and what we don't collect (anything personal you enter into a simulator).

1. Information We Do Not Collect

All calculators on ZAQORI are client-side JavaScript applications. When you enter values into a simulator, those values are processed locally in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

This means we do not have, and cannot obtain, the financial figures, weight, sleep hours, study times, or any other personal data you enter. We don't log inputs, we don't store them, we don't share them.

2. Information We Do Collect

Anonymous Analytics

We use privacy-friendly, anonymized analytics to understand which simulators and articles are popular, which devices and browsers are used to access the site, and to identify technical issues. This data is aggregated, contains no personally identifiable information, and cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

Theme Preference

We store your light/dark mode preference in your browser's localStorage. This is purely functional — it lets the site remember your choice between visits. You can clear it at any time.

Contact Form Submissions

If you voluntarily submit the contact form, we collect the name, email, topic, and message you provide. We use this solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any marketing list, and we delete correspondence after resolution unless a legal retention requirement applies.

3. Cookies

ZAQORI uses a minimal number of first-party cookies and localStorage entries. We do not use third-party advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or cross-site tracking technologies. Our cookies are functional only — they remember your theme preference and basic interface state.

4. Third-Party Services

ZAQORI may include links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We may also use third-party analytics and CDN services to deliver the site. These services are bound by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure hosting, and regular software updates. Because we collect virtually no personal data, the impact of any potential security incident is inherently limited.

6. Children's Privacy

ZAQORI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has submitted data through our contact form, please email us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data we hold. For the data we do collect, you can exercise these rights by emailing zaqori.official@gmail.com. Since simulator inputs are never transmitted to us, those rights are automatically satisfied.

8. International Users

ZAQORI is operated from multiple jurisdictions. By using the site, you understand your data may be processed in countries with different data protection laws. We apply the protections described in this policy regardless of where processing occurs.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be highlighted on the homepage for at least 30 days.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email zaqori.official@gmail.com or use the contact form. We respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
