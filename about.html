About ZAQORI

We built the tool we wished we had — a way to see where today's small choices lead in 1, 5, 10, or 30 years.

Our Mission

Most people understand that habits matter, but the connection between today's small choices and tomorrow's outcomes is invisible. We save a little, but we don't see the decade of compound growth. We exercise occasionally, but we don't see the year-over-year strength curve. We read a chapter before bed, but we don't see the 30 books that adds up to over a decade.

ZAQORI was built to make that connection visible. By turning inputs into charts, timelines, and projections, we help you see — really see — the long-term impact of your daily decisions. Once you can see it, you can change it.

The Story

ZAQORI started as a personal spreadsheet. The founder, frustrated by abstract advice about "compound interest" and "small consistent actions," built a simple calculator to project what his modest savings would look like in 20 years. The number that came back — far higher than he expected — changed how he thought about money. He built similar projections for fitness, reading, and learning. Friends asked for the tool. ZAQORI is the polished, public version of that original spreadsheet.

What We Believe

Numbers beat motivation. Willpower fades. Goals get blurry. But a chart that shows your projected future is hard to ignore. We believe in giving people tools that produce truth, not flattery.

Privacy is non-negotiable. Your financial situation, weight, sleep habits, and reading speed are deeply personal. Every calculator on ZAQORI runs entirely in your browser. We don't have a database of your data because we never collect it.

Free should mean free. No premium tier. No "upgrade to see your full results." The whole platform — all 20 simulators and every article — is free forever.

How It Works

Every simulator uses a real mathematical model. Compound interest uses the standard formula financial planners use. Calorie balance uses the energy equation that nutritionists rely on. Habit formation uses peer-reviewed research on behavioral consistency. We don't pull numbers out of thin air — we use the same models professionals use, wrapped in an interface anyone can understand.

Our Team

ZAQORI is built by a small team of designers, developers, and writers who care deeply about the intersection of technology and human potential. We work asynchronously across time zones, ship improvements weekly, and respond to every email we receive.
